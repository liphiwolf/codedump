Help page

Here, on our "Help page" you will find information about our content and applications.
If you have any further questions please contact us at: imflugzeugfliegen@info.com

Sign up

Email: Please enter a valid email address. Make sure everything is spelled correctly.
First name: Please enter your first name, which is on your passport.
Last name: Please enter your first name, which is on your passport.
Password: Please enter a password with at least 7 characters. Special characters are allowed.
Make sure the password is strong and if possible do not use it for any other login.
Confirm password: Please re-enter the chosen password here to confirm it.
Make sure it matches the entered password. Do not use copy-paste to avoid confirming an incorrect password.

Login

If you have already registered, please enter the appropriate email address and password.
You will then be forwarded to the home page.

Logout

To log out, press the logout button located at the top of the menu bar.
You should see a notification, which confirming that you are logged out.
You will then be automatically forwarded to the login page.

Reservation

To make a reservation, please select the desired aircraft.
You can then make the seat reservation by tapping on the desired seat.

Cancel reservations

To cancel a reservation, please select the relevant reservation and press "cancel reservation".
